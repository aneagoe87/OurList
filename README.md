# OurList: Smart Shopping Lists

A smart shopping list app for **iOS** and **macOS** that works with your family budget.

## Features

- **Organized lists** — Create shopping lists with estimated prices, stores, and categories
- **Budget aware** — See remaining budget from OurBudget as you add items
- **Barcode scanning** — Scan products to look up prices and add items instantly
- **Shopping mode** — Check off items as you shop with a running total
- **Budget optimizer** — Prioritize items by need/want/luxury and see what fits your budget
- **Price history** — Track prices over time with automatic estimates on future lists
- **Family sync** — Shared CloudKit data with OurBudget for seamless family budgeting
- **Checkout** — Auto-log transactions to OurBudget when you finish shopping
- **Multi-language** — English, Romanian, and French
- **Privacy first** — All data stored in your private iCloud account; no third-party tracking

## Pricing

- **Free** — Up to 3 active lists with 10 items each, budget awareness
- **Pro** ($4.99, one-time) — Unlimited lists and items, checkout, optimizer, price history

## Requirements

- iOS 18.0+ / macOS 15.0+
- iCloud account (for sync)
- [OurBudget](https://github.com/aneagoe87/OurBudget) (optional, for budget integration)
