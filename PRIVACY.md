# Privacy Policy

**OurList** ("the App") is developed by Andrei Neagoe. This policy describes how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection

OurList does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device and in your private iCloud account.

### What the App stores

- Shopping lists, items, and price history
- Store names and addresses you enter
- Product catalog data (names, barcodes, prices)
- Your Apple ID identifier (for authentication only)

### iCloud Sync

The App uses Apple's CloudKit to sync data across your devices and with family members via a shared iCloud container. This data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### Community Product Database

When you scan a barcode, the App may contribute the product name and price to a shared CloudKit public database to improve suggestions for all users. This data contains no personal identifiers.

### Open Food Facts

The App may query the [Open Food Facts](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/) public database to look up product information by barcode. No personal data is sent in these queries.

## Third-Party Services

- **Apple CloudKit** — Data sync and storage
- **Apple Sign in with Apple** — Authentication
- **Apple StoreKit** — In-app purchases
- **Frankfurter API** — Currency exchange rates (European Central Bank data)
- **Open Food Facts** — Product barcode lookups

No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Family member profiles for children are managed by parents and stored only in the family's private iCloud container.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by removing the App and clearing your iCloud storage. Uninstalling the App removes all local data.
